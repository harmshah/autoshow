About The Event

Gerogian Auto Show 2023, is not just about showcasing cars, its a platform for industry leaders and experts to share their knowledge and insights. Its not just for the car enthusiasts - it's a family friendly experience that everyone can enjoy. Kids can explore the kids zone activities and enjoy them whole day. Its for the people looking for the unforgettable experience that combines technology, design and innovation. From electric and hybrid cars to luxury sports cars, our event features something for everyone. Visitors can also explore interactive exhibits and demonstrations, including test drives and virtual reality experiences. You can expect to see latest car models, concept cars and and cutting-edge technologies from the world's leading atomotive companies like BMW, Bentley, Yamaha and many more.

Know Our Student Directors

Welcome to the Georgian College Auto Show – Canada’s largest outdoor new car automotive event. This year’s theme is “Drive the Future.” We chose this theme to help represent our students and the future of the industry. Our students are passionate, driven and very excited about what the future holds for the automotive industry. The Auto Show showcases Canada’s automotive sector in a non-pressure, sales-free environment for consumers. Manufacturers are provided with the unique opportunity to demonstrate the latest developments in the automotive industry with the help of its future leaders – our Automotive Business School of Canada students. We have worked very hard to improve the show this year. Our most notable change is the complete re-design of our site layout, as well as the addition of several new events and vendors to captivate our guests throughout the weekend. Please see our schedule of events on page 7 for a full description of what to expect. Thank you for visiting and for your ongoing support of this great community-based event!
