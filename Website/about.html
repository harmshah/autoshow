Message from Georgian College President and CEO

Welcome to the 2023 Georgian College Auto Show. As community members, college partners and industry leaders, you play an integral role in the continued success of the show and our students. Thank you for your ongoing support. Heartfelt gratitude to the Automotive Business School of Canada Board of Directors, our faculty and staff for all they do to ensure our students experience excellence every step of the way in our programs, including in the production of this outstanding annual event. This 100 per cent student-driven show functions as an educational learning lab unlike any other. Organizers have been hard at work again this year, accelerating the event to a new level. The theme – Drive the Future – reflects the innovations you’ll see and the key role Georgian students and graduates have to play as the industry continues to evolve. You can expect the return of show favourites, such as the Pfaff Porsche autocross track, the Plug N Drive electric experience, the Show and Shine and ever-popular live auction on Saturday. All this, in addition to 21 automotive brands showcasing their 2018 models and new technology! It’s no small feat to pull off an event of this scale and size. I am so proud of our students for all they have accomplished. Congratulations!
